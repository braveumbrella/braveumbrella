Auditions for Temporarily Ever After

Saturday, April 7 from 3-5pm

Oregon Contemporary Theatre

Written by Grant Thackray.

Fennel works at (TRU)LUV-KISS, a phone hotline in the land of fairy tales. She spends her days taking calls from characters who need help. However, working this job isn’t her fairy tale wish. But wishing for change doesn’t actually make it happen. Also, who is Jack, and why does he keep calling the hotline to curse himself? Fennel must make her own dreams come true, while keeping this stranger alive.
