Mission Statement

To create bold theatre that excites contemporary artists and audiences. We constantly strive to explore new works, ideas, and ways of approaching old concepts.

Goals

  • To create/showcase new works
  • To have space for improv
  • To make a different kinds of theatre
  • To bring theatre to new audiences
  • To push boundaries
  • To create new ideas
  • To not wait for permission
  • To normalize the weird
