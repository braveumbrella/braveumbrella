We need support

Brave Umbrella is working on putting out its first staged production this Fall and we are asking you to help fund the production and bring it to life.

It is an original play written by David Gallagher and Juliet Rutter called To Own a Dali. It is about a group of people who attend a festival to be free, love each other, and listen to some great music.

The piece explores the experiences of being in a relationship without judgement: polyamorous, monogamous, or single.
